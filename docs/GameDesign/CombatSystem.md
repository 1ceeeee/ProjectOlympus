# ⚔️ Project Olympus - Combat System

## Combat Overview

Project Olympus uses an action-based combat system where players control powerful heroes and fight mythical creatures, enemy factions, and legendary bosses.

Combat focuses on:

- Skill timing
- Player movement
- Hero abilities
- Elemental strategies
- Team combinations

---

# 🗡️ Basic Combat

Each hero has:

## Normal Attack

A basic attack using the hero's equipped weapon.

Examples:

- Sword strikes
- Bow attacks
- Magic projectiles
- Spear combos

---

## Heavy Attack

A stronger attack that deals more damage but requires longer recovery time.

Used for:

- Breaking enemy defenses
- Staggering enemies
- Creating openings

---

## Dodge System

Players can avoid attacks through movement.

Perfect dodges reward players with:

- Temporary speed boost
- Counter attack opportunity
- Energy recovery

---

# ✨ Ability System

Every hero has three types of abilities:

---

## 1. Active Skill

A powerful ability with a cooldown.

Examples:

Lyra:
"Thunder Slash"

- Creates a lightning blade
- Damages multiple enemies

---

## 2. Ultimate Ability

The hero's strongest attack.

Requires energy gained through combat.

Examples:

Kael:
"Flame of Olympus"

- Summons sacred fire
- Burns enemies over time

---

## 3. Passive Ability

A permanent effect that improves the hero.

Examples:

Orion:
"Moon Hunter"

- Increased damage at night

---

# 🌈 Element System

The world contains six main elements:

## ⚡ Lightning

Strong against:

- Water enemies
- Machines

Weak against:

- Earth

---

## 🔥 Fire

Strong against:

- Ice
- Undead creatures

Weak against:

- Water

---

## 🌊 Water

Strong against:

- Fire

Weak against:

- Lightning

---

## 🌑 Darkness

Strong against:

- Divine enemies

Weak against:

- Sacred energy

---

## 🌟 Light

Strong against:

- Corrupted creatures

Weak against:

- Darkness

---

## 🌍 Earth

Strong against:

- Lightning

Weak against:

- Wind attacks

---

# 👹 Enemy Combat

Enemies have different behaviors:

## Normal Enemies

Basic creatures found around the world.

---

## Elite Enemies

Stronger versions with special abilities.

---

## Boss Enemies

Large enemies requiring strategy.

Examples:

- Gorgon Sisters
- Abyssal Titans
- Void Colossus

Defeating enemies rewards players with:
