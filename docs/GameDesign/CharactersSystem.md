# 🧙 Project Olympus - Character System

## Character Overview

Heroes are the main playable characters in Project Olympus.

Each hero has unique:

- Combat style
- Weapon type
- Element
- Skills
- Story
- Growth path

Players build teams by combining different heroes.

---

# ⭐ Hero Attributes

Every hero has six main stats:

## ❤️ Health (HP)

Determines how much damage a hero can take.

---

## ⚔️ Attack (ATK)

Determines damage dealt to enemies.

---

## 🛡️ Defense (DEF)

Reduces incoming damage.

---

## ⚡ Energy

Used to activate Ultimate abilities.

---

## 🎯 Critical Rate

Chance to deal critical damage.

---

## 💥 Critical Damage

Increases damage from critical attacks.
