# 💰 Project Olympus - Economy System

## Economy Overview

The economy system allows players to collect resources, upgrade heroes, craft equipment, and prepare for greater challenges.

Players earn rewards through:

- Combat
- Exploration
- Quests
- Boss battles
- Events

---

# 💎 Currency System

## Gold

**Main Currency**

Used for:

- Equipment upgrades
- Skill upgrades
- Buying items
- Crafting

Gold is earned from:

- Enemies
- Quests
- Rewards

---

## Divine Crystals

**Premium Currency**

A rare resource connected to the power of Olympus.

Used for:

- Unlocking special items
- Summoning heroes
- Limited events

---

## Ancient Tokens

**Special Currency**

Obtained from:

- Boss battles
- Dungeons
- Divine Trials

Used for:

- Rare equipment
- Legendary materials
- Exclusive rewards

---

# 🛡️ Equipment System

Equipment improves hero power.

Types:

## Weapons

Increase:

- Attack
- Skill damage
- Elemental power

Examples:

- Celestial Blade
- Starfall Bow
- Lost Spear

---

## Armor

Increase:

- Health
- Defense
- Resistance

---

## Artifacts

Special items containing ancient power.

Effects:

- Increase critical damage
- Improve abilities
- Boost elemental attacks

---

# ⭐ Equipment Rarity

Equipment has different tiers.

## Common

Basic equipment.

Easy to obtain.

---

## Rare

Improved equipment with bonus stats.

---

## Epic

Powerful equipment with special effects.

---

## Legendary

Ancient weapons connected to heroes and gods.

---

## Divine

The strongest equipment in existence.

Created from lost power of Olympus.

# ⚖️ Economy Philosophy

The economy should reward progress without making players feel forced to grind.

Every resource should have a purpose.

Every reward should help players become stronger.
