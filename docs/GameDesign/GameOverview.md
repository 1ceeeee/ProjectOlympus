# 🎮 Project Olympus - Game Overview

## Game Title

Project Olympus

---

# 🏛️ Game Concept

Project Olympus is an action RPG adventure game inspired by Greek mythology.

Players explore a world where the gods have disappeared and humanity must survive without divine protection.

The player takes control of heroes who discover forgotten powers, ancient relics, and the truth behind Olympus' fall.

---

# 🎯 Game Genre

**Primary Genre:**
Action RPG

**Secondary Elements:**
- Exploration
- Character progression
- Boss battles
- Mythology
- Fantasy adventure

---

# 🌍 Game Setting

The game takes place in a world after the disappearance of the Olympian gods.

Ancient ruins, forgotten temples, dangerous creatures, and powerful factions compete for control of lost divine energy.

The player travels across:

- Ancient kingdoms
- Forgotten forests
- Lost temples
- The Underworld
- The remains of Olympus

---

# ⚔️ Core Gameplay Loop

Players will:

1. Explore the world
2. Complete quests
3. Fight enemies
4. Collect resources
5. Upgrade heroes
6. Discover ancient secrets
7. Defeat powerful bosses

---

# ⭐ Main Features

## Hero System

Players collect and develop unique heroes with different:

- Weapons
- Abilities
- Combat styles
- Elements
- Stories

---

## Combat System

Fast-paced battles combining:

- Normal attacks
- Special abilities
- Dodging
- Elemental powers
- Team strategies

---

## Exploration

Players discover:

- Hidden locations
- Ancient artifacts
- Secret quests

## Story

The main mystery:

"What happened to the gods of Olympus?"

The player's choices will determine the future of the world.

---

A dark fantasy interpretation of Greek mythology.

The world combines:

- Ancient architecture
- Divine magic
- Forgotten civilizations

---

# 🎯 Player Goal

The ultimate goal is to uncover the truth of Olympus and decide whether the world needs gods again.

The future belongs to humanity.
