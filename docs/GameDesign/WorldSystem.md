# 🌍 Project Olympus - World System

## World Overview

The world of Project Olympus is an open fantasy world filled with ancient civilizations, forgotten temples, dangerous creatures, and divine mysteries.

Players explore the remains of a world once protected by the gods.

---

# 🗺️ World Map

The world is divided into major regions.

Each region contains:

- Story quests
- Enemies
- Resources
- Hidden secrets
- Boss battles

---

# 🏛️ Region 1: The Forgotten Kingdom

**Theme:** Ancient Civilization

The first region players explore.

Once a prosperous kingdom protected by Olympus, it is now filled with abandoned cities and lost temples.

### Features:

- Beginner quests
- Ancient ruins
- Training enemies
- First boss battle

---

# 🌲 Region 2: The Emerald Wilds

**Theme:** Nature and Mystery

A massive forest protected by ancient spirits.

Creatures awakened after Artemis disappeared now roam the land.

### Features:

- Hunting quests
- Hidden caves
- Rare materials
- Forest monsters

---

# 🌊 Region 3: The Abyssal Coast

**Theme:** Ocean and Storms

A dangerous coastline controlled by creatures from the deep sea.

The people here search for Poseidon's lost relics.

### Features:

- Sea exploration
- Ship travel
- Underwater ruins
- Sea monster bosses

---

# 🔥 Region 4: The Burning Wastes

**Theme:** Fire and War

A destroyed battlefield where ancient wars were fought.

Many warriors search for the lost weapons of Ares.

### Features:

- Combat challenges
- Weapon crafting
- Elite enemies
- War relics

---

# 💀 Region 5: The Underworld Gate

**Theme:** Death and Darkness

The entrance to the realm of the dead.

The barrier between life and death is becoming unstable.

### Features:

- Undead enemies
- Dark quests
- Rare artifacts
- Final story events

---

# 🏛️ Region 6: Olympus Ruins

**Theme:** Divine Mystery

The remains of the gods' home.

Only the strongest heroes can enter.

### Features:

- Legendary treasures
- Final bosses
- Divine secrets
- Endgame content

---

# 📜 Quest System

Players complete different types of quests.

## Main Quests

Progress the story.

Example:

Discover what happened to Olympus.

---

## Side Quests

Optional missions that reveal:

- Character stories
- World history
- Hidden locations

---

## Daily Quests

Repeatable activities that reward:

- Gold
- Materials
- Experience

---

# 🏰 Towns and Settlements

Safe areas where players can:

- Upgrade equipment
- Accept quests
- Buy items
- Meet characters
- Recover health

---

# 🏚️ Dungeons

Special areas designed for challenges.

Dungeon types:

## Ancient Ruins

Explore forgotten temples.

## Monster Lairs

Fight powerful creatures.

## Divine Trials

Test heroes worthy of Olympus.

---

# 🚀 Travel System

Players can travel through:

- Walking
- Mounts
- Ships
- Teleportation points

Fast travel points unlock after discovering locations.

---

# 🌟 Exploration Rewards

Players discover:

- Hidden treasures
- Ancient relics
- New heroes
- Crafting materials
- Secret lore

---

# World Philosophy

The world should reward curiosity.

Every forgotten temple, hidden path, and ancient ruin should contain a story waiting to be discovered.
