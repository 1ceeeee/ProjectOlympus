# 🎨 Project Olympus - Art Plan

## Art Direction Overview

Project Olympus follows a dark fantasy interpretation of Greek mythology.

The visual style combines:

- Ancient Greek architecture
- Divine magic
- Forgotten civilizations
- Mythical creatures
- Epic fantasy battles

The world should feel mysterious, powerful, and ancient.

---

# 🧍 Character Art

Each hero should have a unique identity.

Character designs include:

- Unique silhouette
- Signature weapon
- Special effects
- Distinct clothing
- Personal color theme
