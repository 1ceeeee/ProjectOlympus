# ⚔️ Heroes and Legends

## The First Heroes

After the disappearance of the gods, humanity searched for warriors capable of protecting the world.

These heroes became known as the **Olympian Guardians**.

---

# 🗡️ Lyra — The Last Guardian

**Class:** Warrior  
**Weapon:** Celestial Blade  
**Element:** Lightning

A young warrior who carries a fragment of Zeus's power.

Lyra searches for the truth behind the disappearance of Olympus.

---

# 🏹 Orion — The Shadow Hunter

**Class:** Ranger  
**Weapon:** Starfall Bow  
**Element:** Moonlight

A mysterious hunter blessed by Artemis.

He protects the wild lands from creatures awakened by the Archon's corruption.

---

# 🔥 Kael — The Flame Bearer

**Class:** Mage  
**Weapon:** Eternal Flame Staff  
**Element:** Fire

The last person able to control Hestia's sacred flame.

His power may be the key to restoring the gods.

---

# ⚔️ Marcus — The Warborn

**Class:** Berserker  
**Weapon:** Ares' Lost Spear  
**Element:** Earth

A warrior searching for the missing weapons of the gods.

His strength comes from surviving countless battles.

---

# 🌑 Nyx — The Forgotten Daughter

**Class:** Assassin  
**Weapon:** Shadow Daggers  
**Element:** Darkness

A mysterious fighter connected to the Underworld.

Her origins are unknown, even to herself.

---

# The Prophecy

"When Olympus falls silent, five heroes shall rise.

Not to serve the gods...

But to decide their fate."
