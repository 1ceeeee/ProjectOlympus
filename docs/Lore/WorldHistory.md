# 🌍 World History

## The Age of Creation

Before mortals walked the earth, the world of Olympus was shaped by ancient forces known as the Primordial Beings.

They created the seas, skies, mountains, and the first civilizations.

## The Age of Gods

The Olympian gods ruled over humanity for thousands of years.

Zeus controlled the heavens.
Poseidon ruled the oceans.
Hades guarded the underworld.

Humanity prospered under divine protection.

## The Great Silence

One day, the gods vanished.

No war.
No prophecy.
No explanation.

Their temples became ruins, their names became legends, and humanity was left alone.

## The Rise of the Archon

Centuries later, a mysterious force known as the Archon appeared.

It sought the power left behind by the vanished gods and began awakening ancient enemies.

Humanity must now uncover the truth behind the disappearance of Olympus.
