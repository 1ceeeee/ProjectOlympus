# 🐉 Creatures of Olympus

After the gods disappeared, ancient creatures hidden from humanity began awakening.

Some were created by divine power.

Others were corrupted by the Archon's influence.

---

# 🐺 Lycan Beasts

**Type:** Monster  
**Origin:** Ancient Curse

Once humans who were transformed by a forgotten curse.

Lycan Beasts possess incredible strength and speed, becoming more powerful under the full moon.

### Weakness:
Sacred weapons and divine energy.

---

# 🐍 The Gorgon Sisters

**Type:** Boss Enemy  
**Origin:** Ancient Myth

Three powerful creatures who survived from the old world.

Their gaze can turn warriors into stone, and their blood contains mysterious magic.

### Weakness:
Attacking from darkness and avoiding direct eye contact.

---

# 🌊 Abyssal Titans

**Type:** Sea Monster  
**Origin:** Ocean Depths

Massive creatures awakened after Poseidon's disappearance.

They destroy coastal civilizations and guard forgotten treasures beneath the sea.

### Weakness:
Lightning-based attacks.

---

# 🔥 Infernal Shades

**Type:** Corrupted Spirit  
**Origin:** Underworld

Souls twisted by the Archon's power.

They are neither alive nor dead and hunt those who possess divine relics.

### Weakness:
Sacred flames.

---

# 🦅 The Celestial Guardians

**Type:** Divine Creature  
**Origin:** Olympus

Ancient protectors created by the gods.

Some remain loyal to Olympus, while others have become lost after centuries without guidance.

### Weakness:
Unknown.

---

# 👑 The Archon's Creation

## The Void Colossus

**Type:** Final Boss

A giant creature created by combining forgotten divine energy with dark magic.

The Void Colossus represents the Archon's dream:

A world without gods.

### Power:
- Reality distortion
- Darkness manipulation
- Divine absorption

### Goal:
Destroy the last connection between Olympus and humanity.
