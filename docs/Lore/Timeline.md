# ⏳ Timeline of Olympus

The complete history of the world before and after the disappearance of the gods.

---

# 🌌 The Age of Creation

**Thousands of years ago**

The Primordial Forces shaped the world.

The oceans, skies, mountains, and life itself were created.

The first humans appeared and began building civilizations.

---

# ⚡ The Age of Olympus

**The Divine Era**

The Olympian gods ruled over the world.

Humanity flourished under their protection.

The gods created sacred relics, powerful weapons, and hidden realms.

During this era:

- Zeus ruled the heavens
- Poseidon controlled the seas
- Hades protected the Underworld
- The Olympians guided humanity

---

# 🏛️ The Golden Age of Humanity

**The Rise of Kingdoms**

Mortal civilizations expanded across the world.

Great cities were built, warriors trained, and legends were born.

Humans began to question:

"Do we need the gods forever?"

---

# 🌑 The Great Silence

**The Fall of Olympus**

The gods suddenly disappeared.

No battle was witnessed.

No explanation was given.

The connection between Olympus and humanity was broken.

Divine relics were scattered across the world.

---

# ⚔️ The Age of Chaos

**The Years After Olympus**

Without the gods, ancient creatures awakened.

Monsters returned.

Kingdoms fought over forgotten divine artifacts.

The Archon Dominion rose from the shadows.

---

# 🔥 The Awakening

**Present Day**

A strange energy begins spreading across the world.

Ancient temples activate.

Lost weapons return.

The descendants of heroes begin awakening their powers.

The prophecy of the five heroes begins.

---

# 🌅 The Future of Olympus

The world stands at a crossroads.

The heroes must decide:

- Restore the gods
- Create a new age for humanity
- Destroy divine power forever

The fate of Olympus rests in mortal hands.
