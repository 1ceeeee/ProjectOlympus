# ⚡ The Gods of Olympus

## The Olympian Pantheon

The gods of Olympus once ruled the world, guiding humanity through the ancient ages.

They were powerful, but even gods were not immortal to time.

---

# ⚡ Zeus — The Sky King

**Domain:** Sky, Thunder, Justice

The ruler of Olympus and protector of mankind.

Zeus carried the legendary Thunderbolt, a weapon said to contain the power of creation itself.

Before his disappearance, Zeus left behind a warning:

*"When the heavens fall silent, darkness will rise."*

---

# 🌊 Poseidon — Lord of the Seas

**Domain:** Ocean, Storms, Earthquakes

The guardian of the oceans.

Poseidon controlled the tides and protected ancient civilizations from destruction.

After the gods vanished, the seas became unpredictable, and forgotten creatures returned from the depths.

---

# 🔥 Hestia — Keeper of the Flame

**Domain:** Fire, Home, Life

The first guardian of humanity.

Unlike the other gods, Hestia cared little for power. She protected mortal kingdoms and kept the sacred flame alive.

Many believe her flame is the last remaining connection to Olympus.

---

# ⚔️ Ares — God of War

**Domain:** Battle, Strength, Conflict

A warrior god feared by enemies and respected by soldiers.

Legends say Ares disappeared while fighting an unknown enemy beyond the mortal world.

His lost weapons became relics sought by warriors.

---

# 🌙 Artemis — Goddess of the Hunt

**Domain:** Moon, Nature, Hunters

Protector of forests and creatures.

After the disappearance of Olympus, Artemis continued watching humanity from the shadows.

Some hunters claim they still hear her voice during the night.

---

# 💀 Hades — Keeper of the Underworld

**Domain:** Death, Souls, The Underworld

Guardian of the realm of the dead.

Hades was never truly evil. His duty was to maintain balance between life and death.

When the gods disappeared, the barrier between worlds began weakening.

---

# The Mystery of Their Disappearance

No mortal knows what happened to the gods.

Some believe they were destroyed.

Some believe they sacrificed themselves.

Others believe they are waiting for a chosen hero to awaken them.
