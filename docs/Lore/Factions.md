# 🏛️ Factions of Olympus

The world after the disappearance of the gods is divided between powerful factions.

Some seek to restore Olympus.

Others want to replace the gods forever.

---

# 🛡️ The Olympian Guardians

**Alignment:** Good  
**Leader:** Unknown

A group of warriors chosen to protect humanity.

They search for ancient relics and uncover the truth behind the gods' disappearance.

### Goal:
Restore balance to the world.

---

# 🌑 The Archon Dominion

**Alignment:** Evil  
**Leader:** The Archon

A mysterious organization created after the gods vanished.

The Archon Dominion believes humanity no longer needs gods and seeks to control all divine power.

### Goal:
Absorb the remaining power of Olympus.

---

# ⚔️ The Iron Kingdom

**Alignment:** Neutral

A powerful human civilization built through technology and warfare.

They do not worship the gods and believe mortals should create their own destiny.

### Goal:
Become independent from divine influence.

---

# 🌲 The Ancient Tribes

**Alignment:** Neutral Good

Old civilizations that still follow the forgotten ways of nature.

They protect sacred lands and ancient knowledge left behind by Artemis.

### Goal:
Preserve the balance between humans and nature.

---

# 💀 The Underworld Covenant

**Alignment:** Unknown

A mysterious faction connected to Hades and the realm of the dead.

They believe death is part of the natural order and must not be controlled.

### Goal:
Maintain the barrier between life and death.

---

# The Coming War

As the power of Olympus returns, these factions must choose:

Will they restore the gods?

Destroy them?

Or take their place?
